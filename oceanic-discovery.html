The duration of the standard tours varies depending on the package you choose. Full-day tours typically last around 6-8 hours, while half-day tours last approximately 3-4 hours.

We use a variety of boats for our tours, including spacious vessels equipped with all the amenities for your comfort and safety.

You can contact Shell Island Beach Tours via email or phone for further assistance or inquiries. Our friendly staff are here to help answer any questions you may have.
