Frequently Asked Questions

Can I bring my own snorkeling gear?

While we provide snorkeling gear as part of the tour package, you're welcome to bring your own if you prefer.

Are there any discounts available for group bookings?

Yes, we offer group discounts for parties of ten or more. Contact us for more information on group booking rates and special perks.

Can I bring food or beverages on the tour?

You're welcome to bring food and beverages on the tour, but please be mindful of any waste and dispose of it properly to help preserve the island's natural beauty.

Are there any additional fees or taxes not included in the tour price?

The tour price includes all fees and taxes, so there are no additional charges beyond the advertised price.

What tour options are available at Shell Island Beach Tours?

We offer a variety of tour options, including full-day tours, half-day tours, snorkeling excursions, and private charters.
